Cinnamon Roll

Description

You don't have to run to the mall to satisfy your craving for cinnamon rolls - soft and warm from the oven and drizzled with icing. We'll show you step by step how to make cinnamon rolls from scratch - from making the dough to filling, forming, baking, and icing - with loads of helpful tips along the way.

Ingredients

  • Yeast dough
  • Butter
  • Cinnamon Sugar
  • Preferential fillings
  • Icing

Steps

  1. Prepare the yeast dough.
  2. Use a rolling pin to rollout the dough.
  3. Spread soft butter over surface.
  4. Sprinkle cinnamon sugar.
  5. Add any other fillings.
  6. Tug the lip of the dough up and over the filling.
  7. Pinch to seal the seam.
  8. Cut into equal pieces and place rolls into baking pan. ETC
